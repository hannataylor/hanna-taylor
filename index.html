About me

Hi! I'm Hanna and I'm a Full Stack Web Developer living in New York City.


I love working in Ruby in Rails and everything that back end web development entails, and I also love design and working in HTML/CSS with JavaScript/jQuery and making the back end come to life.


I'm always looking to improve my skills. I learned Ruby, Ruby on Rails, Javascript, and HTML/CSS in just a few months, so I'm excited to see what I put my mind to next...PHP and Scala are definitely next on the to-learn list.

Projects

Localeyes

Localeyes is an app that connects tourists to day trips curated by locals. Localeyes is built in Rails and utilizes the Google Maps and Google Places API. I worked a lot on front end, implementing Materialize CSS, a framework based on Google's Material Design philosophy. In addition to front end, I created other features on the back end, such as favorites, "been there", "available for tours", and mutual followers.

localeyestrips.com

Potluckr

Web application built in Rails to facilitate Potluck event planning where users can host a potluck, and invited users can mark themselves as attending and sign up for items to bring. Built in a weekend as a fun side project to teach myself Rails as well as Bootstrap.

Film Festival

A mock film festival website with films, categories, and weighted ratings, built in Rails. Designed by myself with just vanilla CSS.

Fuel My Wanderlust

A simple mock travel inspiration website built to utilize Booking.com's Affiliate Partner products.

fuelmywanderlust.com

Travel Overflow

A Stack Overflow clone, built in Ruby and Sinatra where users can ask travel related questions, answer questions, and comment on questions, answers, and other comments.

Quizzy

A flashcard app built with Ruby and Sinatra. Users can play flashcard decks and check their stats on the games they've played.
