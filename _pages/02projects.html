Projects

Localeyes

Localeyes is an app that connects tourists to day trips curated by locals. Localeyes is built in Rails and utilizes the Google Maps and Google Places API. I worked a lot on front end, implementing Materialize CSS, a framework based on Google's Material Design philosophy. In addition to front end, I created other features on the back end, such as favorites, "been there", "available for tours", and mutual followers.

localeyestrips.com

Potluckr

Web application built in Rails to facilitate Potluck event planning where users can host a potluck, and invited users can mark themselves as attending and sign up for items to bring. Built in a weekend as a fun side project to teach myself Rails as well as Bootstrap.

Travel Overflow

A Stack Overflow clone, built in Ruby and Sinatra where users can ask travel related questions, answer questions, and comment on questions, answers, and other comments.
